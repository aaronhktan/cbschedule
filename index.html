Configuration Page

This is the settings page for the CB Schedule app. Fill out the fields, then tap the save button to send them back to your watch!
Your schedule
Wakeup
This allows the app to open automatically at 12:00AM, to refresh what day it is and to push Timeline pins without having to open the app manually.
Export and Load
In order to export your schedule(s), tap the "Generate" button and keep the text somewhere safe. To load past schedules, paste the text (that was previously generated) into the text box and then tap "Load".
